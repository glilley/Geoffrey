Privacy Policy

Like millions of other sites you've likely visited, Huit.ly uses Google Analytics.

Ahh, What does Huit.ly track?

Huit.ly uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous information such as IP address, operating system, browser type, how long you stayed for, and how you got here, which is used to aid in marketing, site management, and product enhancements. Your anonymous data will never be linked to any personal data.

How does Google Analytics track me?

Google Analytics uses first-party cookies to track visitors interactions. Cookies are used by millions of sites and contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive and is used to store information such as the time of visit, whether the visitor has been to the site before, and what site referred the visitor to the web page. Browsers do not share first-party cookies across domains.

Google collects all of this information anonymously. Personal information is information that identifies you, such as your name, email address, or billing information, or other data which can be reasonably linked to such information. Google Analytics prohibits the tracking or collecting of this information or associating personal information with web analytics information.

Want to opt out of tracking?
